Campus Map

UW campus is huge. It is important to know how far away your next lecture is. The lectures are usually 10 min apart if taken consecutively. You should also consider the foot traffic as there are other students going to their classes, which will slow you down.

Type in the buildings and press the 'Get Time' button which will redirect you to Google Maps of buildings of your choices to show you the time and directions.

Note: Add "hall" or corresponding building type next to the building name to increase precision. (e.g Willow Hall, Savery Hall, Odegaard Library)
