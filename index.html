Hello There!

Explore UW is a website created to help current and prospective UW students to get to explore University of Washington. We offer a couple of different services that we, as UW students, felt that it would have been helpful when we were first years.

  • Walk Time Calculator: calculate the time it takes to travel between lecture halls and important buildings or place at UW.
  • Major Info: an overview of the UW major system and summary of the admission requirement for each majors, so that you can plan ahead.
  • FAQ: frequently asked questions (FAQ) about UW, which are from student sources like the r/udub Reddit and from students themselves.
We are working to add more features that can improve student life and allow students to have better experience at UW.If you have any questions or feedbacks, please email one of the creators on About Us page or leave a feedback on Feedback page.

Explore UW is created by UW students; for UW students. This website is run by students, not the University of Washington.
