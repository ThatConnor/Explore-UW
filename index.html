Explore UW

Hello There!

Explore UW is a website that helps students, mainly first-years who are new to campus, navigate their way around. We offer a couple of different services, such as a campus map and distance calculator for your classes, an overview of the major system and how to pick a major that’s right for you, and also frequently asked questions (FAQ) about the school pulled directly from student sources like the r/udub Reddit and from students themselves.

Explore UW is created by UW students; for UW students. This website is run by students, not the University of Washington.
